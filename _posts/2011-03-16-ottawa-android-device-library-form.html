Wish you could just borrow a device for an hour so you can debug a minor issue? Well, we're going to be creating a device library where you and fellow Android developers can add their devices to a device library. Although it isn't necessary to add your email address and twitter account, we do suggest that you have at least one so that other developers can get in contact with you if they need to test against your device.
