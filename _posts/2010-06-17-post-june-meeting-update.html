We also had a presentation from Louis Michon, talking about an android application/tool he is interested in creating. The tool is called Convoy. It is a keypad replacement tool, designed to increase input efficiency, and make  input easier. The concept is to remove all the vowels from the keyboard and only show the consonants. Convoy would then intelligently match the the partial word with the appropriate vowels to complete it. Mr. Michon would like to hear from any interested developers or people seeking more information about the tool. You can contact him at l.michon@sympatico.ca.
